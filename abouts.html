We Build for People

Masterson & Richards Construction Associates

IMasterson & Richards Construction Associates (MCR) is a leading name in the construction industry, recognized for its dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainable practices. With decades of combined expertise, MCR specializes in delivering large-scale infrastructure, commercial, and residential projects that stand the test of time.At MCR, we believe every structure is more than just concrete and steel — it’s about creating lasting value for communities and future generations.

Our portfolio includes modern residential complexes, commercial plazas, highways, bridges, and industrial facilities, all executed with the highest standards of safety, precision, and durability.

Driven by our core values of trust, excellence, and innovation, MCR continues to shape skylines and transform spaces, ensuring every project reflects our vision of building better, smarter, and stronger. -
